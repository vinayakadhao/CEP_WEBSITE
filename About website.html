About the Website

Smart Agricultural Assistance is a digital platform designed to help farmers make better decisions by providing real-time, accessible, and reliable agricultural information. It aims to be a one-stop destination for resources related to weather, government schemes, crop suggestions, soil conditions, and market trends.
