Choosing the right crop for your region can drastically improve productivity and profitability. Crop Suitability refers to the assessment of the land and climate to recommend which crops will grow best under given conditions.

Why Crop Suitability is Important

  • Maximizes yield and income
  • Reduces crop failure risk
  • Minimizes input costs (fertilizer, irrigation)
  • Supports sustainable farming
  • Improves decision-making with science-backed data

How is Crop Suitability Determined?

  • Soil Type: Texture, fertility, drainage capacity
  • Rainfall and Irrigation: Annual precipitation, water availability
  • Temperature and Climate: Minimum and maximum temperature, growing season
  • Topography: Slope, elevation

Technologies Involved

  • GIS and Remote Sensing
  • Weather Data Integration
  • Soil Testing & AI Recommendations
