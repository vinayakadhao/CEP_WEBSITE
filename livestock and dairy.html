ЁЯРД Livestock & Dairy

Livestock and dairy farming are critical to India's rural economy. They provide not just milk, meat, and eggs, but also employment, organic manure, and financial stability to millions of farming families.

Importance of Livestock and Dairy

  • Source of daily income and nutrition through milk, eggs, and meat
  • Improves farm sustainability with organic manure and draft power
  • Acts as an asset during financial distress
  • Significant contributor to rural employment and GDP
  • Complements crop production for integrated farming systems

Opportunities in Livestock Farming

  • Dairy Farming: Cow and buffalo milk production, A2 milk, chilled supply chain
  • Poultry Farming: Layer (eggs) and broiler (meat) businesses
  • Goat and Sheep Farming: Fast-growing meat industry
  • Piggery: Profitable for tribal and rural areas
  • Fodder cultivation: Forage crops, silage, and hydroponic feed

Government Support & Schemes

  • Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)
  • National Livestock Mission (NLM)
  • Rashtriya Gokul Mission
  • Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)
  • National Program for Dairy Development (NPDD)
