Efficient irrigation is crucial for sustainable farming, especially in water-scarce regions. This section introduces various irrigation technologies and methods that save water, improve yields, and ensure proper soil moisture.

Types of Irrigation Systems

  • Drip Irrigation: Delivers water directly to plant roots тАУ best for conserving water.
  • Sprinkler Irrigation: Mimics rainfall, suitable for a variety of crops.
  • Surface Irrigation: Traditional method using gravity flow in fields.
  • Micro-Irrigation: Includes drip and mini-sprinklers for precision.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collects and stores rainwater for future irrigation use.

Benefits of Modern Irrigation

  • Up to 70% water saving compared to flood irrigation
  • Better root zone moisture and nutrient delivery
  • Reduced weed and pest issues
  • Improved crop productivity and soil health
  • Subsidies available under PMKSY (Per Drop More Crop)
