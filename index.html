About the Project

This project was developed under the subject Community Engagement Project (CEP). Our aim is to help Indian farmers by providing them with live insights such as:

  • Weather Forecasts
  • Crop Suitability Recommendations
  • Minimum Support Price (MSP) Data
  • Government Schemes in Hindi, Marathi & English
  • Market Rates and Resources
