About Us

Smart Agricultural Assistance is a student-driven initiative developed as part of the Community Engagement Project (CEP) at PCCOE. Our goal is to empower farmers with real-time access to reliable agricultural informationтАФbe it crop selection, market trends, government schemes, or modern farming tools. We believe that technology should simplify lives, especially for those who feed the nation. This platform bridges the gap between rural communities and digital resources, delivering content in user-friendly formats and regional languages. Through this, we hope to enhance productivity, sustainability, and confidence among India's farming communities.
