Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

"Har Khet Ko Pani" - Launched July 1, 2015

Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) aims to provide comprehensive irrigation solutions to farmers. It focuses on improving irrigation sources, distribution, and micro-irrigation systems like drip and sprinkler irrigation under the slogan "Per Drop More Crop".

Key Assistance Provided

  • Water Management: Financial aid for pipes, farm ponds, tanks, dug wells, borewells, and water lifting devices. Small/marginal farmers receive higher aid.
  • Micro-Irrigation: Up to 55% assistance for small/marginal farmers and 45% for others for drip/sprinkler systems.
  • Other Support: Pump sets, rain guns, and soil management assistance are also available.
  • Funding Amounts: Assistance varies by region, farmer category, and project size.

Detailed Assistance & Cost (English)

  • Water carrying pipes: ₹50/m (HDPE), ₹35/m (PVC), ₹20/m (laminated flat tube). Max: ₹15,000/farmer
  • Drip irrigation (Oil Palm): As per PMKSY guidelines
  • Water harvesting (plastic/RCC tanks): ₹20 lakhs/unit (plain), ₹25 lakhs/unit (hilly areas)
  • Farm ponds/well excavation: ₹1.5 lakhs (plain), ₹1.8 lakhs (hilly)
  • Sprinkler set (Pulses, Wheat, Millets): ₹10,000/ha or 50% of cost
  • Pump sets (up to 10 HP): ₹10,000/unit or 50% of cost
  • Borewell/well excavation: 100% of cost (Max ₹30,000)
  • Mobile rain gun (Pulses only): ₹15,000 or 50% of cost
  • Shallow tubewell: 100% of cost (Max ₹12,000)

  • पाईपलाइन: HDPE ₹50/मीटर, PVC ₹35/मीटर, फ्लॅट ट्यूब ₹20/मीटर (कमाल ₹15,000)

  • जल संचयन: सादा भाग ₹20 लाख, डोंगराळ भाग ₹25 लाख
  • शेततळे/विहीर: ₹1.5 लाख (सादा), ₹1.8 लाख (डोंगराळ)
  • स्प्रिंकलर सेट: ₹10,000/हेक्टेयर किंवा 50%
  • पंपसेट: ₹10,000 किंवा 50%
  • बोरवेल/विहीर: 100% खर्च (कमाल ₹30,000)
  • मोबाइल रेन गन: ₹15,000 किंवा 50%
  • उथळ ट्यूबवेल: 100% खर्च (कमाल ₹12,000)
