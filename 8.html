Crop Insurance Schemes in India

Financial Protection Against Crop Losses

Overview

India offers four major crop insurance schemes to provide financial security to farmers against crop failure and related risks.

1. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

  • Covers food crops, oilseeds, and horticultural crops.
  • Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for annual commercial/horticultural crops.
  • Government shares the excess premium cost.
  • Provides full insured sum on claims and 25% advance for prevented sowing.
  • Remote sensing and drones used for claim settlement.

2. Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS)

  • Similar to PMFBY, but based on weather indices deviation.
  • Premium rates same as PMFBY.
  • Claims are triggered by adverse weather patterns.

3. Coconut Palm Insurance Scheme (CPIS)

  • Designed for coconut palm growers.
  • Premium: ₹9 to ₹14 per palm depending on age.
  • Subsidy: 50–75% for all farmers.

4. Unified Package Insurance Scheme (UPIS)

  • Pilot in 45 districts covering crops, life, assets, and student safety.
  • Integrates PMFBY/WBCIS with PMJJBY, PMSBY, and other insurances.
  • Farmers must choose at least 2 additional sections besides crop insurance.

Who is Covered?

Coverage under PMFBY, WBCIS, CPIS, and UPIS is compulsory for loanee farmers of notified crops and voluntary for non-loanee farmers.

Contact

Visit local bank branches, PACS, cooperative banks, empaneled insurers, or contact your District Agriculture Officer or Block Development Officer. More details available at the official Crop Insurance Portal.

भारत में फसल बीमा योजनाएँ - हिंदी

  • प्रधानमंत्री फसल बीमा योजना (PMFBY): 2% (खरीफ), 1.5% (रबी), 5% (व्यावसायिक/बागवानी)
  • मौसम आधारित फसल बीमा योजना (WBCIS): मौसम सूचकांकों पर आधारित दावे
  • नारियल पाम बीमा योजना (CPIS): ₹9-₹14 प्रति पाम, 50-75% प्रीमियम सब्सिडी
  • एकीकृत पैकेज बीमा योजना (UPIS): 45 जिलों में पायलट, फसल, जीवन, संपत्ति और छात्र सुरक्षा कवर
  • ऋणी किसानों के लिए अनिवार्य, गैर-ऋणी किसानों के लिए स्वैच्छिक
  • निकटतम बैंक शाखा या कृषि अधिकारी से संपर्क करें

भारतातील पीक विमा योजना - मराठी

  • प्रधानमंत्री फसल विमा योजना (PMFBY): 2% (खरीप), 1.5% (रब्बी), 5% (फळबागा/व्यावसायिक)
  • हवामानावर आधारित पीक विमा योजना (WBCIS): हवामान घटकांवर आधारित दावे
  • नारळ पाम विमा योजना (CPIS): ₹9 ते ₹14 प्रति झाड, 50-75% हप्ता सवलत
  • एकात्मिक विमा योजना (UPIS): 45 जिल्ह्यांत पायलट, फसले, जीवन, विद्यार्थी सुरक्षा व इतर कव्हरेज
  • कर्जदार शेतकऱ्यांसाठी अनिवार्य, इतरांसाठी स्वैच्छिक
  • जवळच्या बँक शाखा किंवा कृषी अधिकाऱ्यांशी संपर्क साधा
